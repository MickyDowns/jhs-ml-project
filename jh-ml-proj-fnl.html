Exercising Right: Using sensors to ensure proper form

michael downs

synopsis

Many people measure the amount they exercise (miles run, weights lifted, etc.). Few measure the quality of their exercise, their form. Using data from sensors placed on the belts, forearms, arms, and dumbells of 6 participants, I classify barbell lifts into six classifications inlcuding correct form (class A) and five incorrect forms (classes B-F).
